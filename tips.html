Digitizing Scanning Tips

A practical guide to scanning, splitting, and archiving printed photos quickly.

Step 1: Gather and Sort

Group prints by size for more efficient scanning. Handle prints only by the edges to avoid fingerprints.

Step 2: Scanner Settings

📷 Optimal Settings

  • Resolution: 300 DPI (general) or 600 DPI (archival).
  • Colour: 24-bit Colour (RGB) always.
  • Format: TIFF for masters, JPEG for sharing.

Step 3: Pack the Glass

Place prints face-down. Leave at least a 5mm gap between photos so the algorithm can cleanly identify edges.
