Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 8, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 28, 2026

🔒 Short version: PhotoSplit Studio processes all your images entirely inside your browser. We never receive, store, or transmit your photos. Your images never leave your device.

1. Who We Are

PhotoSplit Studio ("we", "our", or "the service") is a free, browser-based web application available at photosplitstudio.com. The service is provided free of charge and is designed to help users automatically split multiple scanned photographs from a single scanned image file.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

PhotoSplit Studio does not collect, process, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Your photograph files or any images you upload or process
  • The content of any images processed through the tool
  • Personal information such as your name, email address, or location (unless voluntarily submitted via our feedback form)

When you select an image file in PhotoSplit Studio, that file is loaded directly into your browser's memory using the standard browser FileReader API. The image data never leaves your device. All detection, cropping, straightening, and color processing operations are performed entirely within your browser's JavaScript engine using the HTML5 Canvas API and Web Workers.

3. Image Processing — Technical Details

To be fully transparent about how your images are handled:

  • Image files are read using the browser's FileReader.readAsDataURL() method, which creates an in-memory representation accessible only to the current browser tab.
  • All pixel-level analysis (edge detection, bounding box calculation) is performed using CanvasRenderingContext2D.getImageData() — a purely local operation with no network access.
  • Image transformation (straightening, color enhancement, format conversion) is performed by writing to an off-screen HTMLCanvasElement in memory.
  • When you click "Save All" or download a split, the browser generates a temporary object URL (URL.createObjectURL) directly from the in-memory canvas data. No server is involved.
  • When you close or refresh the browser tab, all image data held in memory is immediately and permanently released by the browser's garbage collector.

We have enabled IP anonymisation (anonymize_ip: true) so your full IP address is never stored by Google. Google Analytics does not receive any of your image data. Its privacy practices are governed by Google's own Privacy Policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

PhotoSplit Studio does not set any additional first-party cookies beyond those set by Google Analytics and Google AdSense (described below).

We also use Google AdSense to display advertisements on the site. Google AdSense may set cookies and use similar tracking technologies to serve ads based on your browsing history and interests across other sites. This is standard behaviour for Google's ad network. Google's privacy practices are governed by their own Privacy Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

PhotoSplit Studio is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Data Security

Because all photo processing is performed locally in your browser and no image data is transmitted to our servers, the risk of unauthorised access to your images through our service is eliminated by design. Any personal information submitted via our feedback form is stored in a Google Sheets spreadsheet protected by Google's security infrastructure.
