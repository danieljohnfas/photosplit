How to Take a Good Passport Photo at Home

  1. Background: Stand in front of a plain white wall in a well-lit room. Natural daylight from a window works best — avoid harsh shadows on the background or your face.
  2. Expression: Look directly at the camera with a neutral expression and mouth closed. Eyes must be fully open.
  3. Framing: Your face should fill the centre of the frame. Avoid hats, sunglasses, or anything that obscures your face (medical exceptions apply).
  4. Camera: Any modern smartphone camera set to portrait mode works well. Ask someone else to take the photo — avoid selfies, as the angle is usually wrong.
  5. Upload and size here: Upload the photo above, select your country, use the zoom slider to fill the frame with your face, and drag to centre it perfectly.

How to Print Your Passport Photo

Click Create Print Sheet to generate a 4×6 inch sheet with multiple copies tiled. Print on 4×6 photo paper at home, or upload the sheet file to a pharmacy kiosk (Walgreens, CVS, Boots, etc.) and select 4×6 print size. Cut out the individual photos with scissors after printing.
