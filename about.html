About PhotoSplit Studio

A free, privacy-first browser tool that helps you split multiple scanned photos automatically — no uploads, no installation, no cost.

Our Mission

PhotoSplit Studio was created with one goal: to make digitizing a lifetime of printed photographs as easy and private as possible. Millions of families have shoeboxes full of old prints — memories from birthdays, holidays, and ordinary Tuesday afternoons that deserve to be preserved digitally. The problem has always been the tedious manual cropping step after scanning.

Traditional solutions either require expensive desktop software, upload your private family photos to someone else's server, or demand enough technical skill to make the process inaccessible for most people. PhotoSplit Studio eliminates all three barriers. It runs in your browser, it is completely free, and your photos never leave your device.

How It Works

PhotoSplit Studio runs a multi-pass computer vision pipeline powered by OpenCV.js — the industry-standard open-source computer vision library compiled to WebAssembly and executed entirely inside your browser. When you upload a scanned image, the engine converts it to greyscale, applies Gaussian blur to reduce scanner noise, then runs both Canny edge detection and adaptive thresholding in parallel to robustly locate photo boundaries across a wide range of scan conditions. The two edge maps are combined, closed with morphological operations to bridge micro-gaps, and fed into OpenCV contour extraction to produce precise bounding rectangles for every individual photo detected.

All subsequent processing — auto-straightening, color enhancement, negative inversion, EXIF metadata embedding, and format conversion — is performed using the Web Canvas API and Web Workers, keeping every operation fully local to your device. No image data is ever transmitted over the network. The tool is distributed as a compact set of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript files that can even be saved and used offline after the initial page load.

Supported Features

  • Automatic multi-photo detection powered by OpenCV.js (Canny + adaptive threshold pipeline)
  • Manual crop drawing for custom or tightly-packed regions
  • Auto-straighten for de-skewing rotated photos (±30°)
  • Auto color enhancement for restoring faded and yellowed prints
  • Convert Negative — one-click inversion of scanned film negatives to positive images
  • EXIF metadata year stamping — embeds a custom DateTimeOriginal tag so Apple Photos and Google Photos sort your archive chronologically
  • Social media export presets for Instagram (square & story), TikTok, Twitter/X, Facebook, and WhatsApp — with custom letterbox background and watermark overlay
  • Carousel grid splitter — divides a wide scan into contiguous tiles for Instagram multi-slide posts (3×1, 2×2, 3×3, or custom grid)
  • ZIP batch download — save all splits as a single archive in one click
  • JPEG, PNG, TIFF, BMP, WebP, GIF, and PDF input support
  • JPEG and PNG output with adjustable quality
  • Adjustable background threshold and minimum area detection sensitivity sliders
  • Unlimited undo/redo history for bounding box edits
  • Full keyboard shortcut support for power users
  • Dark mode and responsive mobile layout

Privacy Commitment

We believe your family photographs are among the most personal data you own. PhotoSplit Studio was designed from the ground up to never touch that data. When you open an image file, it is read into your browser's local memory using the standard FileReader API. From that moment, all processing — detection algorithms, image transformations, file exports — happens exclusively inside your browser's JavaScript engine.

We have no back-end servers processing your images. We collect no analytics on which photos you scan. When you close or refresh the browser tab, all image data is immediately released from memory. Read our full Privacy Policy for complete details.
