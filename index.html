Place multiple prints on your scanner and scan them as a single JPEG, PNG, or TIFF file at 300 DPI.

02

Frequently Asked Questions

Do my photos get uploaded to a server?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your photos never leave your device.

What formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, TIFF, BMP, WebP, and even PDF as input.
